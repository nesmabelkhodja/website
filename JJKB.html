Joan Jonas Knowledge Base

Joan Jonas - Organic Honey's Visual Telepathy (1972)


The Joan Jonas Knowledge Base is a database containing the works and relevant information of iconic performance artist, Joan Jonas. As an information resource, it is intended to aid curators, conservators, and others who are researching her work. This project is a part of the Artist Archive Initiative, which was founded at New York University to promote research and conservation of contemporary art. The initiative works directly with the artist, her archive, and people who are familiar with her artistic production.

Who is Joan Jonas?

Joan Jonas is a pioneer of performance and video art. "Jonas works in video, installation, sculpture, and drawing, often collaborating with musicians and dancers to realize improvisational works that are equally at home in the museum gallery and on the theatrical stage. Drawing on mythic stories from various cultures, Jonas invests texts from the past with the politics of the present." (from art21)

The Unique Challenge of Performance Art

Performance art is a unique and interdisciplinary subtext of the fine arts. It combines media, performance, props, and sound. Consequently, it can be rather difficult to document and thus to conserve for academic purposes. Performance art often does not fit into typical art historical classifications of medium and style, nor does it fit into standard archival descriptions. Unlike a painting, which can be represented with a photo of the piece and a description of the medium used, performance art has many distinct pieces.
